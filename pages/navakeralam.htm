At a Glance

Nava Keralam - the word itself has the dream beauty of making the hopes of an entire state real. The present state of Kerala, which once was the land of flowing honey and milk, is quite different. It is in such an atmosphere that the govt. of Kerala laid the foundation for the Nava Kerala Mission. Nava Kerala Mission is a comprehensive mission which can influence positively in all sectors of life including agriculture, education and health.

Ardram

Ardram is a project under the NKM which aims in increasing the health standards. Ardram aims to convert PHCs, CHCs, District Hospitals, Government Medical Colleges and selected AYUSH Kendras into advanced treatment centres. In this way our health sector standards can attain a phase where patients will be treated considering their requirements.
