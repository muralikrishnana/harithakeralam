December is a beautiful time of cold weather and end of the year holidays. People get set to bid goodbye to the year 2016 with new hopes, aspirations and resolutions for 2017. And we hope that this is the right occasion for the launch of dream project - Haritha Keralam Mission. We the government of Kerala do think that this indeed is the best new year gift for Keralites.

This great mission started its journey on the eighth of December 2016 and has received a great response. The mission aims at cleaning Kerala and restoring its natural beauty. Being different from other projects, Haritha Keralam is a peoples' mission.

Pinarayi VijayanKJ YesudasDr. Usha Titus IAS

Haritha Keralam Mission also aims at washing off plastic wastes from Kerala. All the grama panchayaths are advised to ban plastic in their reach and to make people aware of its necessity. People a re requested to use paper bags for shoppings and use plastic bags only where they are un-evittable.

Inaugural Ceremony - Planting Paddy

As a part of making awareness among all people of Kerala an extensive campaign will be held as part of the Haritha Keralam mission. The campaign will also include quiz, painting and essay writing competitions for children. All the 14 districts will work jointly for the success of the mission. The mission is headed by the Chief Minister of Kerala, has KJ Yesudas as the mission ambassador and Dr. Usha Titus IAS as the mission secretary.

Join with us in this massive mission, FOR A BRIGHTER FUTURE, FOR A GREENER NATURE...
