Inaugurating the workshop here on Saturda, Health Secretary Usha Titus said there should be a public health approach to minimising health risks from wide use of radiation in medicine
"Evalution of possible health hazards among healthcare workers is needed. No comprehensive study has been done in Kerala in this area,"she said.
She statedthat the recent discovery of radioactive material in the Mayapuri industrial area of WestDelhi had pointed to the need for radiation protection laws.
