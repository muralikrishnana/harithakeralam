Haritha Keralam Mission

'Haritha Keralam Mission' is one among the four missions launched by the governement of Kerala as a part of the 'Nava Keralam Mission'.The other three are namely 'Ardram', 'Life' and 'Comprehensive Educational Rejuvenation Programme'.The Main initiative of this Mission is to the development of the State & to intensify the position of Kerala on the map.

Haritha Keralam Mission

Haritha Keralam Mission is a comprehensive program for creating new development perspectives by maximum utilization of opportunities through waste management, water resources and resources and agricultural development using organic farming. The mision was launched as an initiative to save Kerala from day by day increasing non bio-degradable waste and to ensure that Kerala's beauty remains untouched for the future generations to reside in.

Haritha Keralam Mission is a project of and for the people of Kerala. This massive movement aims to clear and remove wastes from all water bodies in the state, ensure the proper disposal of solid waste and removal of waste water and to increase the area of land under cultivation.

Haritha Keralam Mission aims at starting its journey from the grass root level - the panchayaths - of Kerala. Vacant lands will be found, cleaned and will be used for vegetable cultivation under the supervision of the panchayaths and the municipalities. The panchayths and municipalities have also been directed to implement a well furnished and comprehensive waste management plan to collect, seperate and recycle waste.

Power generation from solar energy is a part of this project. This will be implemented under Kerala State Electricity Board. Other than this 'swap shops' are to be set up at 93 places across the state where reusable products like mobile phones, electronic products etc. can be conserved and handed over to another free of cost. Unnecessary e-waste production can be checked by this to an extent.

Timeline



Haritha Keralam Mission was officially launched on December 08, 2016, under the leadership of the Hon'ble Cheif Minister of Kerala, Pinarayi Vijayan.

The state level inauguration was done at Kalatharaykkal of Kollayil panchayath of Thiruvananthapuram. Mission ambassador KJ Yesudas sang the 'Haritha Kerala Geetham', penned by poet Prabha Varma, at the ceremony.


Yesudas singing Haritha Kerala Geetham at the inaugural ceremony

Following this Chief Minister, along with Yesudas and chief guest Manju Warrier, handed over to farm workers the paddy to be planted in the fields which have been lying fallow till the previous year.
